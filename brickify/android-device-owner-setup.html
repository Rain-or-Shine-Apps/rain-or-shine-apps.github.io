Android setup — Device Owner mode

Brickify's real Android protection needs one extra, one-time step: a factory reset and a short guided setup. This page walks through it.

← Back to Brickify

Android's "Device Admin" permission — the quick, no-reset option — can always be switched off by anyone with physical access to the phone, via Settings, with no password needed. That's too easy to defeat for what Brickify is for, so the supported setup goes one step further: Brickify installed as this phone's Device Owner, which has no such Settings escape hatch at all. It takes a bit more effort once, in exchange for protection that actually holds.

⚠️ Before you start: is this phone managed by an employer or school?

Device Owner mode and existing organisation management (a "Work profile," or IT department control) are mutually exclusive on Android — this setup will not work on a managed device, and attempting it on a fully-managed corporate phone will fail outright.

Check Settings for a "Work profile" section, or ask your IT department. If this phone is managed, use a personal, unmanaged device instead.

Setup

1

Confirm you've bought Brickify

If you tapped "Buy Brickify" from inside the app already, you're set — note which Google account you used, you'll need to sign into that same one later. If you haven't bought it yet, open Brickify on the phone first and follow the prompt there before continuing.

2

Open this page on a second device

You're about to erase this phone, so you won't have its screen available once the reset starts. Read through to the end here, on your own phone or a laptop, before you begin.

3

Factory reset the phone

Settings → System → Reset options → Erase all data (factory reset) — exact wording varies by phone manufacturer. This erases everything currently on it.

4

Reveal the QR scanner

On the blank "Welcome" screen that appears after the reset, tap the same spot on the screen 6 times in a row. A QR code scanner opens.

5

Scan this code

The phone needs internet access to download Brickify — connect to Wifi if prompted. It installs and sets itself up as Device Owner automatically, with no further taps needed.

6

Finish setup, then Restore Purchases

Brickify opens on its own once provisioning finishes. Sign into the same Google account you used to buy Brickify, then go through setup as normal (PIN, second factor, profiles, schedule). At the final "Ready to take control of your phone?" screen, tap Restore previous purchase instead of paying again.

Why the purchase carries over: your payment is tied to your Google account through Play Billing, not to the specific copy of the app installed on this exact phone. Restoring finds that same purchase and unlocks Brickify again — no second charge.

Troubleshooting

  • Restore Purchases doesn't find anything — double check you're signed into the same Google account used to buy Brickify. Still stuck? Email rain.or.shine.apps@gmail.com with your device ID (shown in Setup) and we'll sort it out.
  • QR scanner won't open — you need a genuinely blank, freshly-reset phone; it only appears on the very first "Welcome" screen, before any account is added.
  • Scan succeeds but nothing installs — the phone needs internet access to download Brickify. Connect to Wifi during the setup wizard if you weren't prompted automatically.
  • Settings shows a "Work profile" and this didn't work — see the warning above; Device Owner mode can't coexist with organisation-managed device settings.
