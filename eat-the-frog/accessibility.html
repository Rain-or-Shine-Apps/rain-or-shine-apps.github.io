We want Eat The Frog to work well for everyone. Here is what the current version supports.

👁 Screen Reader (VoiceOver & TalkBack)

Eat The Frog supports VoiceOver (iOS) and TalkBack (Android). Buttons, the frog task card, and the celebration screen all include descriptive labels — for example, the "eat it" button announces the task title and whether it's already been completed.

iPhone / iPad — VoiceOver Settings → Accessibility → VoiceOver
Android — TalkBack Settings → Accessibility → TalkBack

⚡ Reduce Motion

Eat The Frog checks your device's Reduce Motion setting and responds to it accordingly.

iPhone / iPad Settings → Accessibility → Motion → Reduce Motion
Android Settings → Accessibility → Remove Animations

🌗 Light & Dark Mode

Eat The Frog uses a fixed light theme and does not follow your device's dark mode setting.
