Accessibility

Pocket Muney by Rain or Shine Apps

← Back to Pocket Muney

Pocket Muney follows your device's accessibility settings automatically — there's nothing to configure in the app itself.

🌗 Light & Dark Mode

Pocket Muney follows your device's system appearance setting.

iPhone / iPad Settings → Display & Brightness → Light / Dark
Android Settings → Display → Dark Theme

🔤 Text Size

The app automatically respects your device's text size settings.

iPhone / iPad Settings → Display & Brightness → Text Size
Android Settings → Display → Font Size

👁 Screen Reader

Pocket Muney supports VoiceOver (iOS) and TalkBack (Android). All buttons and chore tiles include descriptive labels — for example, a chore button announces its name, reward amount, and whether it's already been completed today.

iPhone / iPad — VoiceOver Settings → Accessibility → VoiceOver
Android — TalkBack Settings → Accessibility → TalkBack

⚡ Reduce Motion

Pocket Muney checks your device's Reduce Motion setting and minimises animations when it's turned on.
