Pocket Muney

Making chores rewarding! Turn chores into Carrots, celebrate Pay Day, and watch good habits grow.

iOS & Android — Coming Soon

Pocket Muney is the fun, family-friendly app that turns chores into something your children actually want to do.

Instead of reminding your kids for the tenth time, let Pocket Muney do the motivating. Set up chores worth a certain number of Carrots — our friendly fictional currency — and watch your children race to tick them off. When you're ready to pay out, tap Pay Day and watch the coins fly!

Carrots can mean whatever works for your family — pocket money, screen time, a treat, or simply the satisfaction of a job well done. You decide.

😊

Multiple children

Manage all your kids from one app. Switch between them with a tap — each child has their own balance and chore list.

🧹

Custom chores

You choose the chore name and how many Carrots it's worth. Assign different chores to different children, or the same ones to everyone.

⭐

One-tap logging

Tap a chore to award Carrots instantly. Tap again if they did it more than once. Balances update automatically.

📋

Unpaid Chores list

See every completed chore that hasn't been paid out yet. Swipe left to remove a log if needed.

✏️

Manual additions

Add a custom Carrot amount any time — useful for bonus rewards or those lazy-parent moments.

Pay Day

When the balance is ready, tap Pay Day for a satisfying coin explosion! The full payout is saved automatically in Pay History.

📜

Pay History

A complete log of every payout, with an expandable breakdown showing exactly which chores earned what.

🌟

Star Chart — Coming Soon!

Another brilliant way to reward your children — the classic star chart, reimagined with the ease of the Pocket Muney app.
